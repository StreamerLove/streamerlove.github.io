About StreamerLove


StreamerLove is a twitter account which retweets almost any streamer we can find.
StreamerLove isn't like most, we don't use any sort of script to retweet streams. We do it by hand.
The people behind StreamerLove don't fully like to confirm who they are. Only real way to know who we are is by others saying it or us giving out hints we won't fully say this is us.
